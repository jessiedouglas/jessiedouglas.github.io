About Me

I am a web developer based in New York. Born and raised in the beautiful countryside of West Virginia, I attended Oberlin College, a small college located near Cleveland, OH, where I studied mathematics. After graduating, I moved to North Carolina to attend Duke University and pursue a career as a high school math teacher. It was while attending Duke that I took my first coding course and fell in love with it. After a year working as a full time teacher, I decided that teaching was not for me, and I came to New York to pursue my new-found love of computer programming. Although I am new to the profession, I am a quick learner and very excited to be in the field!

Make sure to check out my projects page to see some of the projects I have already built, and take a look at my resume!
